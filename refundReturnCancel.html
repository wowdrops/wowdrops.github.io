Customer Satisfaction Guarantee

At Wowdrops, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to deliver premium quality canned drinking water and seamless subscription services right to your doorstep.

01

Returns & Replacements

Damaged or Unsatisfactory Deliveries

If you are not entirely satisfied with the quality of the product delivered, or if you receive a damaged/leaking water can, you can return it immediately.

To initiate a return or replacement, please contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery. You can reach us via email at wowdropsfoodandbeverages@gmail.com or call/WhatsApp us directly at +91 70928 88999. Upon verification, our delivery executive will collect the product and arrange for a fresh replacement or initiate a refund.

02

Cancellation Policy

Order & Subscription Cancellations

  • On-Demand Orders: You can cancel an on-demand water can order at any time before it leaves our distribution center. Once the order is out for delivery, cancellations cannot be processed.
  • Subscription Plans: If you wish to cancel or pause your ongoing water subscription plan, you can do so through the Wowdrops app or by contacting our customer support line.
03

Refund Processing

How Refunds Work

Refunds will be initiated under valid circumstances, such as canceled orders (prior to dispatch), confirmed product quality issues, or account balance closures for subscription plans.

Credited within 7 Working Days (Bank/UPI/Wallet)
